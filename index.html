About Us

Recycle has been founded to reduces pollution by increasing the efficiency of waste management and reducing pollution of all kinds by spreading a culture of recycling among community members to contribute to environmental conservation, energy and resource management in the best possible way.

We provide a recyclable waste pick-up service from your place. Awarding our customers for each process they complete by calculating points for them, which they can redeem at our cooperating companies.
